Our Story

My grandson recently completed a school project that required us to create a new family tradition. We decided we wanted to use this opportunity to help others. Our new tradition is called Hill's Hope, in honor of our late Uncle who was homeless. Each year at Thanksgiving we will be making "Winter Bags" filled with needed items to hand out to the homeless at Henry's Place in Beaumont, Texas.

According to Keith at Henry's place, 65 - 70 homeless individuals come through the facility daily. Our hope is to be able to provide a bag for each person in need. Our bags will consist of the following items: Pair of socks and gloves, scarf or knit hat, snacks and a wash cloth.
